Portfolio

Rainbow Pictures is a Production House establish in 2019 engaged in providing Pre-Production and post-production services for Advertisements and Numerous alternative media. We cater to varied acknowledged organisations for all their sound as well as Video Editing needs, Voice Overs, Visual and Special effects and much more. Our services portfolio includes the creation of AD Films, Documentary/Short Film, Video Film Production/Video Editing, Non-Linear Editing, Audio Visual Production, Community Radio Setup, Video Coverage, Advertising, Audio Recording/Dubbing and Media Consultancy. Our team has over 20 years of hands-on experience in the field of Media and entertainment. We have delivered over 100 projects including the grand scale projects (4K, 6K, RED etc).

After two decades producing corporate videos, commercials, television shows, and feature films, our expertise has a wide range. We have won numerous awards for our clients over the years and take pride in over delivering.

Lately, we have been doing a lot of work for Real Estate, medical industry, construction industry, corporate events and conferences, explainer videos and reality television. For these videos, we have used conventional shooting, 2D and 3D animation, specialised shooting with jibs, sliders and dollies, and time-lapse.

Rainbow Pictures believes in building long and genuine relationships with all our clients. We believe in long term associations and that is why, from the start we invest our time, efforts and expertise to advise and help our clients to reach their marketing & Business goals.
